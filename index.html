Get Started

The single fastest way to connect with us on is Flock!

Step 1: Join Flock

Flock is a slack-alternative and the premier medium where FCCA Members keep in touch!

Create and join by visiting Flock

  • Announcements - Look here for when FCCA and other important events!
  • General - Make new friends by chatting about coding, events, and related topics!
  • Resources & Local Events - Share with us great resources and events with other members!
  • Daily-Log - Daily coding is a powerful habit. Strengthen this habit by typing 1 sentence about what you coded today!

About

We are a local organized group of aspring developers who strive to learn by working together.

Goal of the Group:

  • Project-driven but open ended
  • Can pair up with others to work on projects during the code and coffee hangout
  • Projects will come from FCC website
  • Later, we hope to have tiny FCCA projects for you to build/finish during the hangout

Scope of the Group:

  • Mostly Front-End Development
  • Focused on HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Other frameworks welcomed

Expectations of Group Members:

  • Have a laptop and an IDE
  • Familiarity with Version Control (Git and GitHub) or we can show you
  • Be familiar with some coding language
